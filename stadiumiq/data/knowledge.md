# MetLife Stadium Operations & Guest Information

## Prohibited Items
To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all guests, several items are strictly prohibited inside MetLife Stadium. These include weapons of any kind, pocket knives, pepper spray, outside food and beverages (except one factory-sealed bottle of water up to 20oz), professional video/audio recording equipment, drones, and self-balancing boards. All guests must pass through security metal detectors, and any prohibited items found will be confiscated.

## Gate Hours
public gates at MetLife Stadium open exactly 3 hours prior to kickoff for most events, including international soccer matches. For example, if kickoff is scheduled for 8:00 PM local time, gates will open to ticket holders at 5:00 PM. Premium suite and club entrances may open 30 minutes earlier. All gates remain open until the end of the first half, after which entry is restricted.

## Bag Policy
The stadium enforces a strict bag policy to expedite entry times. Only clear plastic, vinyl, or PVC bags that do not exceed 12" x 6" x 12" are permitted inside the venue. Additionally, small clutch bags or purses no larger than 4.5" x 6.5" are allowed and do not need to be clear. All other bags, including backpacks, briefcases, and large tote bags, are strictly prohibited and must be returned to vehicles or checked at bag storage lockers.

## Re-entry Policy
Re-entry is strictly prohibited at MetLife Stadium. Once a ticket is scanned at the turnstile and the guest exits the security perimeter, they will not be allowed back in under any circumstances. Exceptions are made only in extreme medical emergencies, which must be coordinated directly through the Guest Services or First Aid desks near the main entrance gates.

## Accessibility Services
MetLife Stadium is fully compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and offers comprehensive accessibility services. Accessible seating is available on all levels and tiers, and elevators are conveniently located at each of the stadium's cardinal concourses (North, East, South, West) for vertical transit. Guests requiring sensory assistance can check out sensory bags containing noise-canceling headphones and fidget tools at any Guest Services booth.

## Lost and Found
Any items lost during an event are turned over to the Lost and Found center located at the Guest Services booth near Gate A on the ground level. Guests can report lost items online via the MetLife Stadium website or visit the physical booth during or after the match. Found items are held in storage for 30 days before being donated or disposed of.
